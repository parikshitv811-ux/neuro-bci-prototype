PHASE 2 — DIRECTION ALIGNMENT LOSS

Create:
tsta_project/training/direction_alignment.py

Compute direction:
d = normalize(e_T - e_0)

Class mean direction (EMA):
μ_class = running mean across all subjects

Loss:
L_align = 1 - cosine(d, μ_class)

Inter-class separation:
L_sep = max(0, cosine(d, μ_other) - 0.2)

Total loss:
L_total = InfoNCE + 0.15*L_align + 0.10*L_sep
